Introduction

The rivalry between Real Madrid and Barcelona goes beyond just soccer. In recent years, it has expanded to include basketball in the EuroLeague. The two teams have a storied history of competing against each other, dating back to the early 20th century.

The first official soccer match between the two teams took place on May 13, 1902, which Real Madrid won 3-1. Since then, they have played over 200 matches, with Real Madrid leading the overall head-to-head record.

In basketball, their first EuroLeague encounter took place in the 1940s. Since then, they have faced each other multiple times in both domestic and international competitions. One of the most memorable games was in the 2013 EuroLeague quarterfinals, where Real Madrid defeated Barcelona 3-2 in a best-of-five series.
